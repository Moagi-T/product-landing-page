ABOUT

Here at Selah Café, coffee is the language we speak. Coffee knows us and we know her, very well you could say. We aim to create a warm, inviting space where people come together to connect, whether it be through meeting new people, or connecting with their innermost selves while you enjoy an exquisite cup of our specialty coffee.

Our minimalist space, featuring earth tones and inspiration from nature calmness, aims to provide the perfect atmosphere for those who seek to ground themselves while enjoying our one of kind coffee. Whether you’re seeking a quiet corner to work, a cozy spot to unwind alone, or an intimate setting to share meaningful time with family and friends, Selah welcomes you with open arms, and warm coffee.

Here we invite you to slow down, connect, and savor life’s little moments while enjoying a delicious warm beverage. Who knows, our coffee might just bring you new life-long friendships.
